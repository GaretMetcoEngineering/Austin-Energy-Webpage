AUSTIN Community Solar Program

Austin Energy

Austin Energy’s community solar initiative allows businesses and property owners to support clean energy, reduce electricity costs, and contribute to sustainability without the upfront cost of traditional installations.

Metco Engineering partners with you to lease rooftop space and install high-performance solar PV systems at no cost. You benefit from passive income, improved environmental branding, and a direct role in Austin’s renewable future.

OUR DETAIL-ORIENTED APPROACH

Austin Energy, in partnership with Metco Engineering, takes pride in a structured and thorough process that ensures the success of every solar project. Our engineering team is committed to evaluating your site and energy needs with precision before installation begins.

In-Depth Goals Assessment

We conduct a comprehensive on-site evaluation to understand the unique energy requirements of your facility. This includes a preliminary feasibility study to assess rooftop viability and system size potential.

Production Simulation Report

Using advanced modeling tools, we generate a simulation report that forecasts the expected energy production and economic impact of your proposed solar system giving you clarity and confidence from the start.
